Lord of the Rings, The Rings of Power, Season 2, Episode 2

A few days ago, while watching television with my father, he decided to continue his watch-through of a controversial prequel to the critically acclaimed movie series. As a fan of the original films and the books, jumping straight into this episode was quite jarring. So many different characters I had heard of before, along with familiar ones in completely new settings. Overall, I enjoyed the interactions between Sauron and Celebrimbor, as well as the meeting with Durin II.
